Quality Leaf

The Quality Leaf is a web application designed to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of leaf collection and payment management between farmers and agents. It offers real-time data entry for leaf collections, allowing agents to log details such as date, type, quantity, and quality grade instantly. Automated processes handle dynamic pricing models, weight, and quality calculations, reducing human error and ensuring consistent data. It generates invoices instantly upon collection logging and provides robust tools for tracking payments, including integration with various payment gateways for seamless transactions. The platform features customized user roles and permissions to maintain data security, making The Quality Leaf a comprehensive solution for streamlined leaf collection and payment operations
